Model Notes
  • Data (live via FRED): Fed Funds (FEDFUNDS), CPI level (CPIAUCSL, computed YoY), Real GDP YoY (A191RL1Q225SBEA), HY OAS (BAMLH0A0HYM2), Oil WTI (DCOILWTICO).
  • Stress propagation (editable in code): ΔEBITDA% = −α₁·ΔCPI − α₂·OAS/100 − α₃·max(0, −GDP) ± oilSensitivity·ΔOil.
  • ΔInterest% = (1 − rateHedge)·[β₁·ΔFedFunds + β₂·OAS/100]. ICR = EBITDA / Interest.
  • PD = σ(θ₀ + θ₁·Leverage + θ₂/ICR + θ₃·OAS + θ₄·RecessionDummy − θ₅·creditHedge).
  • Optimizer: coarse grid over allocations, picks feasible mix with highest Sharpe-like proxy.
